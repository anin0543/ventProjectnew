Ventilation Units

In ventk2 you can set your own airflow

If you are Zealand area you may experience the airflow of 25-30 cf/m in normal day.

Temperature

A safe temperature is accepted to be between 68- and 74-degrees Fahrenheit for people above the age of 65.
If you feel cold you can choose to stay in Warm through ventk2 app.
If you feel warm you can make your climate zone warm.
